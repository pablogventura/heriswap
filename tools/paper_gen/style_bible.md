# Paper craft collage style bible (Heriswap)

## World

Photographed handmade paper craft as if made by primary-school children on a messy wooden school desk. Chaotic, gluey, joyful - not clean Instagram scrapbooking.

## Materials (mix; one dominant + 1-2 accents)

- Matte construction paper (vivid primary-school colours)
- Newsprint (blurry illegible text only as texture)
- Magazine gloss fragments
- Shiny gift-wrap / foil
- Aged kraft / yellowed paper
- Lined or grid notebook paper
- Crooked masking tape, glue stains, overlapping layers

## Edges

- Scissors-cut: imperfect jagged cuts
- Hand-torn: fibrous white paper edge
- Mix both within a single piece when layered

## Characters

Hedgehog stays a hedgehog: body collage, spikes = torn paper strips of mixed papers, eyes = paper circles. Three skins = different material palettes. Branch = kraft/construction strips, not botanical wood.

## Scene / UI

Desk clutter, crooked paper labels for buttons, irregular cut digits, paper-scrap clouds, confetti not realistic snow.

## Lighting

Soft classroom daylight, visible paper grain, soft contact shadows.

## Forbidden

CGI/plastic toys, pure watercolor illustration, neon candy UI, perfect laser cuts, minimal clean scrapbook, readable newspaper headlines on pieces, autumn-leaf-only set.
